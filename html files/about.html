Our Story

From humble beginnings to India's premier online bookstore

Founded in 2023, Bookworld India has come a long way from its beginnings. When we first started out, our passion for books drove us to do intense research, and gave us the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into a booming online store.

We now serve customers all over India, and are thrilled to be a part of the fair trade wing of the publishing industry. Our commitment to quality, authenticity, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in the book industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
